[0xB0, 0x4A, 0x40] turn tuner mode on
[0xB0, 0x4A, 0x3F] turn tuner mode off
[0xC0, MM] (MM in 0x00 to 0x31) change to patch MM
[0xF0, 0x7E, 0x00, 0x06, 0x01, 0xF7] identity request, should return [F0 7E 00 06 02 52 5F 00 00 00 31 2E 30 30 F7]
[0xF0, 0x52, 0x00, 0x5F, 0x50, 0xF7] enable echo messages
[0xF0, 0x52, 0x00, 0x5F, 0x33, 0xF7] request patch info
